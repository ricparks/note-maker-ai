#### Reviews
Released in October 2024, 'Continuum' serves as a reflective companion piece to Ólafsson's highly acclaimed recording of the Goldberg Variations. Critics have described it as a 'coda' that explores the architectural beauty and spiritual depth of Bach's music through a selection of transcriptions and canonic variations. While too recent for historical retrospectives from sites like AllMusic, early reception highlights Ólafsson's characteristic clarity, sensitive touch, and his ability to make Bach's counterpoint feel both modern and timeless. It is praised for its meditative atmosphere, bridging the gap between the rigorous structure of the Goldbergs and a more somber, introspective conclusion.
